Neon Festival Eye Makeup Look

The brief tutorial below will give you an idea of my process. Neon pigments can be difficult to use. The trick is to pack the powder on, and then blend, to avoid any blotchiness.

Start with a bare base

Cover lid with white creme eyeshadow

Add darker shadow to crease

Cut crease with more white shadow

Add lighter shadow to lid

Add glitter for festival fun

Finish with brows and mascara, and voila!
